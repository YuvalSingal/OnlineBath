Our Company
Our company was established in 2022 in Israel.
Our supplier is a professional intelligent bathtub manufacturer with independent research and development, scale production and global marketing.
At present, our company is at is late stages of our product manufacturering and safe testing, and soon our product is going to the market in Israel and United States.
How it started
Our product was inspired by Japan's fasinating 'Onsen'(Hot Spring) culture. One of the effect of this culture on Japan's development was having a unique smart bathtub in many households.
However, this smart bathtub oporation system is limited to the range of the house, and needs a full installation of new bathtub and systems in older households.
Thous, our company developed a different system that can be installed on almost any bathtub, in any household, so every household could install their own smart bath at lower cost.

"We want to introduce Japan's amazing invention to all Israeli households, while also have Israel's inovation touch, and IOT capabilities to improve households' commeditiy even more."(Yuval Singal)
